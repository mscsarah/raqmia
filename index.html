Q2: Propagation Delay & Frequency Calculations

A) A 4-bit asynchronous binary counter. Each J-K Flip-Flop is Positive edge-triggered and has a propagation delay for 10 ns. Calculate the total propagation delay ($t_{p,total}$) and maximum clock frequency ($f_{max}$).

B) An asynchronous binary counter is implemented using 6 flip-flops. Each flip-flop has a propagation delay $t_{pd}$ of 12 ns. Calculate the total propagation delay ($t_{p,total}$) and maximum clock frequency ($f_{max}$).

طريقة الحل: بما أنه عداد غير متزامن (Asynchronous)، فإن التأخير تراكمي. نضرب عدد القلابات في زمن التأخير للقلاب الواحد.

Solution: For asynchronous counters, delay is cumulative. $t_{p,total} = n \times t_{pd}$.

Solution A (4-bit, 10ns):

1. Total Delay: $t_{p,total} = 4 \times 10 \text{ ns} = \mathbf{40 \text{ ns}}$
2. Max Frequency: $f_{max} = \frac{1}{t_{p,total}} = \frac{1}{40 \times 10^{-9} \text{ s}} = \mathbf{25 \text{ MHz}}$

Solution B (6-bit, 12ns):

1. Total Delay: $t_{p,total} = 6 \times 12 \text{ ns} = \mathbf{72 \text{ ns}}$
2. Max Frequency: $f_{max} = \frac{1}{t_{p,total}} = \frac{1}{72 \times 10^{-9} \text{ s}} \approx \mathbf{13.88 \text{ MHz}}$

تُستخدم نظرية دي مورغان لتحويل عمليات العكس المنطقية بين AND و OR والعكس. وهي مفيدة جداً في تبسيط الدوائر المنطقية.

خلاصة:

  • عكس ناتج AND يساوي OR للمدخلات المعكوسة.
  • عكس ناتج OR يساوي AND للمدخلات المعكوسة.

Used to convert logical inversions between AND and OR. Very useful for simplifying logic circuits.

Summary:

  • Inverted AND output equals OR of inverted inputs.
  • Inverted OR output equals AND of inverted inputs.
